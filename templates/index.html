Leveraging AI for Accurate Diagnosis

About Our Project

Our project focuses on developing an AI model to classify cancer cell images accurately. We use state-of-the-art machine learning algorithms and a robust dataset to train our model. Our mission is to enhance diagnostic tools to aid healthcare professionals.

Project Details

We employ convolutional neural networks (CNNs) for image classification. Our process involves data collection, preprocessing, model training, and evaluation.

Results

Our model achieved a 50% - 68% accuracy rate in classifying cancer cell images in the three chategories which are Melenoma, Nevus and seborrheic keratosis.

Conclusions

The results of our project indicate a better future for AI-assisted diagnostics. We aim to continue improving our model and explore on how to improvise accuracy in developing the model to achieve better outcomes.
